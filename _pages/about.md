I am a cognitive psychologist interested in the cognitive and neural mechanisms underlying language processing, bilingualism, and prediction. During my PhD at RWTH Aachen University, I investigated how semantic prediction influences language control during bilingual language switching and how disconfirmed prediction is processed across different language contexts. 
Methodologically, I have extensive experience in experimental design, statistical modeling, and the analysis of behavioral, eye-tracking, and electrophysiological data using R, Python, and MATLAB.

---
## Research Interests
- Prediction in language processing
- Language-specific and domain-general mechanisms
- Bilingualism and language control
- Neurocognition of language
- Cognitive control
- Predictive processing
