# About Me

I completed my PhD in Cognitive Psychology at RWTH Aachen University in December 2025 under the supervision of Iring Koch and Andrea M. Philipp.

My doctoral research, *The Role of Prediction During Language Processing*, investigated how semantic prediction influences language control in bilingual language switching, and how disconfirmed predictions are processed in within- vs. cross-language contexts.

During my master’s training, I focused on bilingual language control, including the use of non-invasive brain stimulation techniques (tDCS) and reward-based mechanisms in language processing.
